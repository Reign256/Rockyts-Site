Welcome to The Rockyts' Fan Site!

This is an unofficial website for the band The Rockyts, and is made to celebrate the music of the band.
The Rockyts used to be a three piece band consisting of Jeremy Abboud on guitar, Keith MacGouganon bass, and Camden McKimm on Drums.
This arrangement changed when Keith decided to focus on studying and left the band. Camden soon followed, with him studying abroad.
This left the band with only Jeremy, who turned the group into a solo project. Find out more on the Band History page on our site.
